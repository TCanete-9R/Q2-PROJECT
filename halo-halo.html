Halo Halo

Ingredients
  • Shaved ice
  • ½ cup sweetened red beans
  • ½ cup sweetened jackfruit strips
  • ½ cup sweetened bananas (minatamis na saging)
  • ½ cup sweetened coconut strips (macapuno)
  • ½ cup nata de coco (coconut jelly)
  • ½ cup leche flan (cubed)
  • ½ cup ube halaya (purple yam jam)
  • Evaporated milk
  • 1 scoop of ube or vanilla ice cream (optional)
Instructions
  1. Layer Ingredients: In a tall glass, layer red beans, jackfruit, bananas, macapuno, nata de coco, and leche flan.
  2. Add Ice: Fill the glass with shaved ice, pressing gently to compact it.
  3. Top and Serve: Drizzle evaporated milk over the ice. Top with ube halaya and ice cream, if desired. Serve with a spoon and mix before eating.
